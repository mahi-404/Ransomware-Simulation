Epstein’s Private Island (Publicly Known Facts)

One of the most discussed locations connected to the case is Little Saint James, a privately owned island in the U.S. Virgin Islands. The island was purchased by Epstein in the late 1990s and later became the subject of extensive media coverage during criminal investigations

According to publicly available court documents and reporting:The island was used as a private residence It contained residential buildings and guest facilities.Law enforcement agencies searched the property as part of legal investigations.
